Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dishcount really free?

Yes! Dishcount is 100% free to use. We don't charge for any features, and we don't take a cut of your grocery purchases.

How often are deals updated?

Deals are refreshed weekly when stores publish new circulars. Kroger deals update in real-time when you connect your account. Most other stores update every Wednesday.

What stores are supported?

We support 80+ grocery chains including Kroger, Walmart, ALDI, Publix, Meijer, H-E-B, Safeway, Sprouts, Food Lion, and many more regional chains.

How does Dishcount build recipes?

Dishcount looks at what's on sale at your selected stores, what you already have at home, your family's dietary needs and taste preferences, and your weekly budget — then builds recipes that maximize your savings while making meals your family will actually enjoy.

Do you sell my data?

Never. We don't sell, share, or monetize your personal data. Your zip code is used only to find nearby stores. If you create an account, your preferences are stored securely in your account. If you don't sign in, your preferences are only used for the current session and aren't saved.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@dishcount.co with a description of the issue, or use the contact form above. Screenshots are always helpful!
