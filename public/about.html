About Dishcount

We believe eating well shouldn't mean spending more.

Our Mission
To help every family eat delicious, home-cooked meals while spending less on groceries — by turning this week's sales into tonight's dinner.

The Problem We Solve

The average American family spends $270 per week on groceries — and throws away nearly 30% of what they buy. Meanwhile, every grocery store runs weekly sales that most shoppers never fully take advantage of.

Dishcount bridges that gap. We scan the weekly ads from 80+ grocery chains, find what's actually on sale at YOUR local stores, and use AI to generate delicious recipes that maximize those deals.

How It Works

1
Enter your zip code and we'll find every grocery store near you — along with their current weekly sales.
2
Browse this week's deals across all your favorite stores. Tap items you want to cook with, and mark any you'd rather skip.
3
Our AI generates 8+ recipes tailored to the items on sale, your dietary preferences, and your chosen cooking style — so you eat great and save money.

Our Commitment

Dishcount is free to use. We don't sell your data, we don't take a cut of your grocery bill, and we never will. Our goal is simple: help families eat better for less.
