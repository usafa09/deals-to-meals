Affiliate Disclosure

Dishcount is a free service. To help fund continued development, we participate in affiliate programs with select grocery retailers and platforms. When you click certain links on our site and complete a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links never affect which deals or recipes Dishcount surfaces — those are determined by your local store data and your preferences, not by which retailers compensate us.

Programs we participate in

We currently participate in (or have applied to) the following programs:

  • Amazon Associates
  • Kroger affiliate program (via FlexOffers)
  • Walmart affiliate program (via Impact)
  • Instacart affiliate program (via Impact)

Affiliate compensation is the only commercial relationship we have with these retailers. We are not paid by any retailer to feature their deals or promote specific products in our recipes.
