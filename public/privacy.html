Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Dishcount LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the websites dishcount.co and dealstomeals.co (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

(a) Information You Provide

  • Your zip code (used for store searches, not permanently stored with your identity)
  • Account information if you register (name, email, dietary preferences, favorite recipe types, preferred store)
  • Saved recipes
  • Contact form submissions

(b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • A session cookie to maintain your login state

We use Google Analytics with anonymized IP addresses to understand how visitors use our site. We do not use advertising pixels or sell data to advertisers.

(c) Information from Third Parties

If you connect your Kroger account, we receive a temporary access token to add items to your cart. We do not store your Kroger password, and we do not access your Kroger purchase history.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Find nearby grocery stores and current deals
  • Generate personalized recipe suggestions using AI
  • Save your preferences and favorite recipes
  • Improve and maintain the Service
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

We explicitly do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Build advertising profiles based on your activity
  • Share your data with data brokers or marketing companies
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing and improving the Service

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy. Each service receives only the minimum data necessary to provide its function.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, which runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure. We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Hashed session tokens (never plain-text)
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Environment variables for all API keys and secrets

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Zip code search results are cached for up to 30 days
  • Deal data is cached for 24 hours
  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Saved recipes are retained until you delete them

If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of Kroger integration at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email support@dishcount.co.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact support@dishcount.co.

9. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies and a minimal analytics service:

Cookie / Service Purpose Duration
sb-*-auth-token Supabase authentication for user login sessions Session
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics — collects anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site, general geographic location, device type) to help us understand how the Service is used and improve it. No personally identifiable information is collected. Up to 2 years

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Third-party tracking pixels
  • Cookie-based user profiling

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
