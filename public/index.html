How we calculate these numbers ▾

80+ grocery chains: Dishcount aggregates weekly deals from over 80 grocery store chains including Kroger family stores (24 banners), ALDI, Walmart, Publix, Albertsons, Meijer, H-E-B, and many more through our weekly ad scanning pipeline.

6,000+ weekly deals: This is the typical number of unique sale items available across all supported stores in a given week. The actual number varies by location and which stores are near you.

$30–$50/week savings: This estimate is based on the average price difference between sale prices and regular prices across a typical weekly meal plan of 5–7 dinners using sale items. Individual savings depend on your location, stores, and what you buy.

50+ recipes per search: Our AI generates recipe suggestions based on your selected sale items and preferences. The number of available recipes varies based on which items are on sale in your area.
