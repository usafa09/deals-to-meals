Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dishcount really free?

Yes! Dishcount is 100% free to use. We don't charge for any features, and we don't take a cut of your grocery purchases.

How often are deals updated?

Deals are refreshed weekly when stores publish new circulars. Kroger deals update in real-time via their API. Most other stores update every Wednesday.

What stores are supported?

We support 80+ grocery chains including Kroger, Walmart, ALDI, Publix, Meijer, H-E-B, Safeway, Sprouts, Food Lion, and many more regional chains.

How does the AI create recipes?

We use Claude AI to analyze your selected sale items and generate budget-friendly recipes that maximize your savings while considering your dietary preferences and cooking style.

Do you sell my data?

Never. We don't sell, share, or monetize your personal data. Your zip code is used only to find nearby stores, and your preferences stay on your device.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@dishcount.co with a description of the issue, or use the contact form above. Screenshots are always helpful!
